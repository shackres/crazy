Rider 2 brings you even more fantastic and fast-paced neon racing action. If you enjoyed the original Rider game, you will simply love this sequel! The music and graphics are just as cool, and the gameplay is just as fun and challenging.

Each level presents a different course to race along. You must control your bike and ensure that it lands with its wheels facing down. This might sound easy, but you must time your acceleration perfectly! See what high score you can gain, and how many awesome neon bikes you can unlock!
